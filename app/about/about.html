Our Mission
NewsNow is a news aggregator that enables users to find articles they are interested in faster. NewsNow consolidates news articles from various news sources including New York Times, BBC, The San Francisco Chronicle, etc. Our mission is to take out the tediousness of searching for interesting and relevant news stories and instead provide a platform that showcases them for you. NewsNow uses machine learning to find articles based on your preferences/interests.
Introduction
NewsNow was developed by a small team of UC Berkeley students through the organization Mobile Developers of Berkeley.
How are we different?
Compared to other news aggregator websites, NewsNow is able to learn which topics and subjects you enjoy and showcase all related articles rather than requiring users to customize their feed. Additionally, we give the user the option to filter which sources or topics are searched providing another layer of user customizability.
